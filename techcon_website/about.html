History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for software developers and has grown into a global technology conference attracting thousands of attendees each year.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, promote collaboration, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders through world-class events and meaningful conversations.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Ada Lovelace - A pioneer in computing, she spoke on the future of AI and ethics in technology.
  • Alan Turing - His talk focused on cybersecurity and cryptography.
  • Grace Hopper - She shared her journey in software engineering and mentorship in tech.
